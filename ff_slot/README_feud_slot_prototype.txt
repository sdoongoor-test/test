Feud Go Slot Balancing Comparison Prototype

Open: feud_slot_vs_category_prototype.html

This package compares two presentation approaches:
1) Pure Slot Symbols: Coin Master-style symbols and match-3 triggers.
2) Category / Mode / Value: explicit category -> mode -> value reel logic.

Balancing presets included in the HTML:
- Coin Master Pacing / Pure Slot: Tests looter rhythm with more breathing room, match-3 anticipation, and Feud presence through full hits plus passive symbol exposure.
- Team V7 Pacing: Tests maximum content visibility with frequent Survey, Showdown, Chance, arcade, and modifier routing.
- Slot / V7 Hybrid: Tests the middle ground: pure slot-symbol presentation with slightly higher content density than Coin Master pacing, but less event spam than Team V7.

Notes:
- Auto Spin is enabled.
- Passive symbol-exposure rows do not trigger popups.
- Hit overlays only appear for full Survey, Feud, or Chance launches.
- Fast Money estimate assumes Survey, Bullseye, and Survey Steal all contribute Survey Points.
